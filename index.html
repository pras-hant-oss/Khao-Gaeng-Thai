Our Story

A Slice of Bangkok
in the Heart of Leeds

Bringing authentic Thai street food culture to Yorkshire since 2019.

The best way to dine in our capital city "Bangkok" is to go to the local Khao Gaeng stall, where an abundance of healthy and delicious street food is served quickly with fresh ingredients. This Bangkok lifestyle has become one of the most attractive things that visitors and tourists love about Thailand, and here it is — a local Khao Gaeng Thai Stall in the heart of Leeds Kirkgate Market!

Every dish is prepared with love using traditional recipes passed down through generations. We source the freshest ingredients daily from the market vendors around us, ensuring every bite transports you straight to the bustling streets of Bangkok.
